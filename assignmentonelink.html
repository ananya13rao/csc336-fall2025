Welcome to Ananya's Rankings!

Here are my ratings for some books I have read - out of 5 stars!



Daisy Jones and the Six by Taylor Jenkins Reid ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

This book receives 5 stars because it was super engaging, interesting, and I never knew what to expect!

This book is about a band in the 60s and their rise to fame, as well as what drama led to their split.



Before the Coffee Gets Cold by Toshikazu Kawaguchi ⭐️⭐️⭐️

This book was a bit boring at times, but had cool descriptions and was still pretty interesting.

This book is about a small coffee shop with the power to make anyone go back in time, under certain circumstances.



Where the Crawdads Sing by Delia Owens ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

This book has a really interesting plot with some unexpected turns!

This book is about a woman who lives on the marsh, and how the town has ostracized her.
